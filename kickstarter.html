Lumina

Rediscover colors and abilities of a colorless world in this 2D puzzle platformer.

Story

Lumina is an atmospheric 2D puzzle platformer with an emphasis on aesthetics. In a world which lost its color, players will travel to solve puzzles, overcome obstacles, gain new abilities, and rescue the colors.

With the rescued color companions, players can use a variety of abilities including dashing quickly to gain distance, double jumping in the air, and more.

Avoid obstacles such as dark brambles, rushing currents, and monstrous plants to find the lost colors and bring life back to a withering world!

We, 2indiegames, are an indie game development group as part of the CSE 481 D capstone class at the University of Washington. We plan to make this 1 indie game, Lumina, in 2D.

We will begin rolling the game out on February 9th with the final product complete by March 10th.

Risks and challenges

In the case of busy school schedules, our rollout schedule may be delayed. However, we will have a complete game by the end.
