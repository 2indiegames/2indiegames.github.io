The Game

Lumina is an atmospheric 2D puzzle platformer with an emphasis on aesthetics. In a world where color has faded, players will travel to solve puzzles, overcome obstacles, and gain new abilities by rescuing the colors.

With the rescued color companions supplying power, players can use a variety of abilities including dashing quickly to gain distance, double jumping in the air, and more.
